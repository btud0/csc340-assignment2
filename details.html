Satoru Gojo

An explanation of Gojo's Character:

Key Details

  • Universe: Jujutsu Kaisen
  • Role: Jujutsu High Teacher
  • The strongest sorcerer in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe.
  • Techniques: Six Eyes, Limitless
  • Personality: Charismatic, Confident, Laidback

Satoru Gojo is the sensei of Jujutsu High. He is the exception to almost every rule in the universe. His power is unmatched and he is the target for every antagonist as he is the only person that can always stop them.
